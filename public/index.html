ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

I acknowledge that this project is produced on the traditional lands of the Wurundjeri people of the Kulin nation. I pay my respects to their Elders past, present, and emerging.

FOREWORD

This project started from a personal exploration of cultural identity and the complex relationship I have with my heritage culture. Past negative experiences had resulted in me carrying a sense of shame and disconnection, and even actively distancing myself from my cultural background. However, over time, being around a supportive community and gaining a deeper understanding of cultural diversity has helped me see cultural identities in more nuanced ways. Iâ€™ve slowly learned to accept myself and take pride in who I am, and in who I have become through these experiences.

Through lost'n found, I wanted to give back to the community. I want to create a resource that can help people relate.
