FOREWORD

This project started from a personal exploration of cultural identity and the complex relationship I have with my heritage culture. For a long time, past experiences had led me to carry a sense of shame and disconnection, and even actively distance myself from my cultural background. However, over time, being around a supportive community and gaining a deeper understanding of cultural diversity has helped me see cultural identities in more nuanced ways.
