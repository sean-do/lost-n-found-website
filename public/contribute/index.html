GET YOUR STORIES HEARD

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the lost ‘n found archive.

This project exists to document the everyday realities of cultural experiences and the diversity and fluidity of cultural identities. By contributing, you’re helping to build a resource that others can connect with, learn from, and find comfort in.

WHO CAN CONTRIBUTE?

Everyone is welcome.

lost ‘n found is a safe and open space for you to be heard, a community built through sharing, listening, and learning together.

Your story might also be selected to be published in the next issue of the lost ‘n found zine!

WHAT DO I WRITE ABOUT?

You can write about anything related to culture and identity, including but not limited to moments of discovery, tension, celebration, or change. Leave a topic (or more) that you have in mind on the form, or if you already know you’d like to write about, a short summary, and I’ll get back to you with more details and next steps.

If you have any questions, contact me at seando.design@gmail.com
